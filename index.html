Driver Requirements

To maintain our high standards of service and safety, all prospective drivers must meet the following criteria and provide valid documentation.

Personal Documentation

  • Valid South African ID or Work Permit
  • Valid Professional Driving Permit (PrDP)
  • Proof of Residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Clean Criminal Record Check

Vehicle Requirements

  • Vehicle Model 2014 or newer
  • Valid Vehicle License Disc
  • Roadworthy Certificate
  • Comprehensive Commercial Insurance
  • Operating License / Double Disc

Device Requirements

  • Smartphone (Android 8.0+ or iOS 13+)
  • Active Data Plan
